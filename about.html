About Us

Our Story

Established in 1982, the Institute for Cultural Research and Action (ICRA) is dedicated to advancing sustainable agricultural practices for small-scale farmers in rain-fed regions. Our primary focus lies in fostering equitable methodologies, facilitating knowledge exchange, and strengthening community-led initiatives to enhance resilience and prosperity in rural communities through a robust environmental and ecological approach.

Our Vision

To foster a future where every farmer prospers, biodiversity flourishes, and sustainable farming practices ensure food and nutrition security and ecological stability for the present and future generations.

Our Mission

Enabling small-scale farmers to adopt sustainable agricultural practices strengthens the farming system's resilience and stability while simultaneously improving ecological security within rural communities.

Our Motto

"Nurturing Growth, Sustaining Communities."
