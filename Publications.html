ICRA Publications: Celebrating the Spirit of Alternative Agriculture

Rooted in Farmer Experiences

ICRA's publications honor the transformative journeys of visionary farmers who have embraced alternative agriculture. By focusing on biographies of pioneers like Shri D.D. Bharamagoudara, Shri Narayana Reddy, and others, we celebrate their technical expertise, commitment to organic farming, and creative spirit. These stories inspire and guide a new generation of farmers.
